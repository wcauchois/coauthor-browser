The Graph Visualization allows the user to see an interactive graph of how the authors are connected.

Using Graph Visualization

  • Right-click on the background of the graph to have it auto-zoom the graph to fill the screen.
  • Use the scroll-wheel on your mouse to zoom in and out on specific parts of the graph.
  • Click and drag on the background to pan across the graph.
  • Click on a node to add its co-authors' nodes to the graph.
    • This will also run a new search on the selected author within the main UI.
  • Enter a name into the search field and all nodes of that name will be highlighted.
  • Hold down 'Shift' and click and drag to zoom in and out of the graph.
