To search the database for a publication by title, use Article Search.

Using Article Search

  1. Start at the search page.
  2. Select the Article Search tab.
  3. Enter your search terms and press enter or click the search button.

You will be taken to a list of publications that match your search criteria.

Tip: To view more detailed information about a publication, right click and select "Search for this publication" from the drop down menu.

Filtering Results by Publication Year

You can use the slider at the bottom of the results page to filter the articles by their year of publication.

  1. Drag the top slider to the right until the desired starting year is selected.
  2. Drag the bottom slider to the left until the desired ending year is selected.

Only articles with a publication year between the starting year and the ending year will be shown. To view all articles again, simply drag the sliders back to their starting positions.
